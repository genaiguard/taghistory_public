TagLogger Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data (latitude, longitude, and timestamps) related to your tracked assets.
  • Account identifiers such as email address and authentication details.
  • Usage and operational telemetry needed to support and improve the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide platform features such as current location, history, geofencing, and alerts.
  • Operate account, billing, support, and service reliability workflows.
  • Improve product quality and troubleshoot issues.

3. Lawful Use Responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring your tracking activities comply with applicable privacy and surveillance laws, including obtaining required consent and notices.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your location data. We may share data only when needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, or protect rights and safety.

5. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active or as needed for service operation, legal compliance, and fraud prevention. We honor deletion requests as required by law.

6. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. No system can be guaranteed fully risk-free.

7. International Processing

Your data may be processed in regions where TagLogger and service providers operate, subject to applicable legal safeguards.

8. Children

The service is not intended for users under 18 years old.

9. Your Rights

  • Access and review account information.
  • Request correction or deletion where legally available.
  • Object to certain data processing activities where applicable.

10. Updates

We may update this policy and publish the revised version with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have data privacy questions, use the contact page.
