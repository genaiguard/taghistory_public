TagLogger Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 8, 2025

Version: 1.0

Introduction

At TagLogger, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services. By using TagLogger, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data Controller: TagLogger is the data controller responsible for your personal information. For any questions or concerns about this policy or your data, please contact us via the in-app support form or through our Contact Us page.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Location Data: Latitude, longitude, and timestamps associated with your AirTags for location history and tracking features.
  • User Identification: Account details like your email address or username to link location data to your account.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Used to authenticate users, remember preferences, and analyze website usage.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the TagLogger service, including location history, geofencing, inventory management, and interactive timelines.
  • Improve our services, troubleshoot issues, and develop new features.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, or support matters.

TagLogger processes location data solely on behalf of the account holder and for the purpose of providing the tracking features you request.

You are responsible for ensuring that any collection or sharing of location data with respect to other individuals complies with applicable privacy and surveillance laws, including obtaining consent where required.

How We Share Your Information

We do not share your location data with third parties, except as follows:

  • Trusted Service Providers: We may share data with trusted vendors (e.g., for hosting, analytics, or payment processing) who are bound by confidentiality obligations and use your data only to perform services for us.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as a subpoena or court order.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect TagLogger, our users, or others.
  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly agree to share your data.

We may suspend or terminate access to the Service or cooperate with law enforcement if we detect or receive credible reports of unlawful tracking activity.

Your Choices and Rights

You can:

  • Access: View your data through your account dashboard.
  • Correct: Update inaccuracies in your account settings.
  • Delete: Request data deletion, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Object: Opt out of certain data processing where permitted by law.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app support form or visit our Contact Us page.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption, access controls, and secure servers to protect your data. However, no system is fully immune to risks.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in other countries, with safeguards to comply with applicable laws.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18, and we will delete such data if discovered.

Business Use

If you use TagLogger for business (e.g., tracking shipments), you must comply with privacy laws and obtain necessary consents.

Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active or as needed for the service. Upon deletion requests, we remove it unless legally required to retain it.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies help with authentication, preferences, and analytics. Manage them via your browser settings, though this may limit functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes via our website. Continued use after updates implies acceptance.
