Where construction equipment tracking breaks down

Tools and equipment move between yards, job sites, and crews daily. When a concrete saw doesn't show up Monday morning, the crew is idle while someone makes phone calls. When a trailer disappears over the weekend, discovery happens too late for recovery.

Industry estimates suggest equipment sits idle 40-60% of the time on typical sites - not because there aren't enough assets, but because nobody knows where they are. TagLogger gives operations and field teams one place to check before the phone chain starts.
