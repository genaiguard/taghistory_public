Frequently Asked Questions

Can we start with only certain asset categories?

Yes. Most teams start with high-impact equipment categories—those that are expensive to replace or that cause the most delay when missing—and expand over time as the workflow becomes routine.

Can operations and field teams share access?

Yes. TagLogger supports collaborative, multi-user workflows. Everyone with access sees the same location and history data so recovery and allocation decisions don’t depend on a single person.

Can this support theft prevention strategy?

Yes. Geofence and movement context help teams identify and respond to suspicious activity faster. When tagged assets leave approved zones, alerts create a chance to act before the asset is gone for good.

What equipment can we track with TagLogger?

Anything that can carry an AirTag: tools, trailers, generators, specialty kits, and mobile equipment. Teams typically start with high-value or high-mobility items that move between sites and are costly when lost or underutilized.
